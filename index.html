Title

Text is a continuous language unit in linguistics that is used in communication as a complex unit of communication. Refers to the grouping of words that together make up a statement. The term is usually used for long-range written statements (paragraph), but it can also be used for spoken language or even short statements. Books are referred to as artistic texts, newspaper articles as journalistic texts, etc.

The text has the following properties

complexity: consists of smaller parts (sentences), continuity: its units (sentences) are interconnected, there is an obvious connection between them,

functionality, informativeness: it is created with the intention by which the author wants to influence the addressee, organisation: its units are organised according to their interrelations, involvement in context and communication situation: the text is designed to be understood by the addressees. It is clear that some of these text properties may be intentionally or inadvertently violated.
